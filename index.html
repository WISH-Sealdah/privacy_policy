Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the WISH (Welfare Inspector Smart Hub) app for Sealdah Division collects, uses, and protects the personal information of internal railway staff. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The WISH app collects and processes the following data for official welfare and administrative purposes:

  • Employee details (Name, Department, Designation, Mobile Number)
  • Form submissions across modules such as:
    • Grievance Forms
    • Beat Inspection
    • Colony Inspection
    • CGA Enquiry
    • Pension Forms
    • ONR Enquiry
    • Misc./Death Enquiry
    • Police/Bank/School Verification
  • Submission History
  • App usage logs for performance improvement

2. Location Data Usage

The app accesses device location only when a user submits a form. This location is recorded to identify where the submission was made from and to support verification of inspection and reporting activities.

  • Location is collected only during active form submission
  • No continuous or background location tracking is performed
  • Location data is visible only to authorized supervisory users
  • Location data is not shared with third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for internal railway administrative purposes, including:

  • Processing and verifying welfare-related forms
  • Scheduling and tracking inspections
  • Sending reminders and notifications
  • Improving app performance and user experience

4. Smart Reminders & Notifications

The app may send push notifications such as scheduled visit reminders, form status updates, and administrative instructions. These notifications are only related to official duties.

5. Data Security

We implement internal security measures including encrypted storage, role-based access, and controlled login to ensure employee data is protected from unauthorized access.

6. Data Sharing

The information collected is used only within the Sealdah Division and associated railway departments. No data is shared with third parties outside Indian Railways.

7. User Responsibilities

  • Maintain confidentiality of login credentials.
  • Ensure forms are submitted with accurate information.
  • Use the app only for official railway welfare operations.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect new features or administrative requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes.

9. Contact Information

For any queries related to privacy or data handling, please contact your Welfare Inspector or the Sealdah Division administration.
